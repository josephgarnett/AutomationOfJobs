Key Findings
There are three main factors that affect the probability of whether a job can be automated: • Social Intelligence - Human emotion is difficult for machines to understand & even harder for a machine to respond to in the appropriate manor • Creativity - Coming up with ideas or artifacts that are novel & valuable. The difficulty here for machines is that creativity is an arcane concept for humans & therefore that makes it hard to encode into machines • Perception & Manipulation - Humans very adept at identifying & handling objects in any environment. For machines to be able to do tasks involving perception and manipulation the work environment has to be designed to accommodate.
